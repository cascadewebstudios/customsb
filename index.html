My name is Advik Sagiraju and I’m based in Seattle, WA. I started CustomSB because I wanted to get my hand-painted skateboards out into the world. I’ve always loved painting and coming up with my own designs, and seeing them come to life on a skateboard is an amazing feeling. Every deck is made by hand, with its own unique vibe and style. I'm excited to keep making more and sharing them with anyone who likes the art.
